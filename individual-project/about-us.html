About CitrusTV

CitrusTV is Syracuse University's entirely student-run TV studio, open to all majors with any level of experience at SU or SUNY-ESF.

All the content broadcast by CitrusTV is written, directed, edited and produced entirely by students. The studio's promotions, advertising, finances, engineering, computer systems, operations and event planning are also all managed by volunteer students.
